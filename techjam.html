EVENT DESCRIPTION :

Participants will be asked to speak on any technical topic chosen by them from the list of topics. Judging will be based on content, vocabulary, representation. Event will be conducted throughout the given time slot.

The event will be conducted in only one round : 11:30 A.M to 2:30 P.M

RULES AND REGULATIONS :
  • On spot registration is not allowed.
  • Only one round will be conducted.
  • Participants will be given the topic on-spot.
  • 10 minutes of preparation time will be given.
  • Each participant can speak only for 5 minutes with the time being monitored by the coordinators.
  • Participants will not be permitted to speak more than once in any case.
  • In-case of any dispute, Judge’s decision remains final.
  • Estimated time taken: 30*5= 150 mins (2 hrs 30 mins).
  • Buffer Time: 30 mins.
  • All the events are limited only to the students of technical institutions.
  • The students are required to bring their college ID cards.
