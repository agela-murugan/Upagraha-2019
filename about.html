I'm sure most of you would have heard about our college SVCE. For those who haven't, Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering (SVCE) is an engineering institution located in Pennalur, Sriperumbudur near Chennai. Upagraha (which means satellite in Kannada) is the symposium associated with the department of Electronics and Communication. Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering has a long-standing history of excellence in undergraduate education with its inception in the year 1985 with an annual intake of 60 students.
